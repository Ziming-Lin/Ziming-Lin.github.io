I am a fan of big data analytics and am currently exploring a mid-career switch. Although my education background is in chemical and biomolecular engineering, I took the plunge to enter Singapore's public service sector early in my career mainly to improve my communication skills. At Housing and Development Board (HDB), I was exposed to many tasks and responsibilities requiring me to communicate with internal and external stakeholders and I believe I have benefitted greatly from my 6-year stint there.

Part of my role at HDB was to tackle mortgage arrears and while the job was secure and comfortable, I found myself facing challenges that could certainly use the help of data analysis. And with the pandemic ongoing then, I left the job to give myself time to work on this skillset as well as my personal fitness which was lacking due to a prior shoulder operation.

Since leaving HDB, I have had many experiences previously unachievable through the lack of time. These include 3D printing and 3D modeling, learning about physiotherapy, opening a webstore, streaming video games, touching the basics of music production, and a few more.
